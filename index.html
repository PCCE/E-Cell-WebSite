Business Idea Competition

Rules:

  • Team should consist of two members maximum. Both should be paid members of the E-Cell.
  • Entries can be submitted in two categories: Technical and Non-Technical.
  • Entries will be shortlisted by CIBA prior to the competition. Teams shortlisted will be informed via email.
  • Time limit given should be adhered to. No extension will be allowed.
  • No foul language or aggressive actions allowed.
  • Judging criteria will be decided by CIBA.
  • A valid email address and mobile phone number must be given with entries.

    • The competition will consist of the following stages:

    Stage 1: Presentation

    Time limit: 7 minutes maximum.
    A slide show using Microsoft PowerPoint, or equivalent program should be presented.
  • No slide limit.
  • Either one or both team members can present.
  • Stage 2 will commence as soon as Stage 1 concludes.

    • Stage 2: Q&A Round

    Time Limit: 3 minutes
  • Questions will be asked by judges, Teachers and Audience.
  • Judges decision is final and binding.

    • Questions to Answer

  • What is the market opportunity? What is the problem (and its size) that you are trying to address?
  • What is your product/service offering? How is it unique/a breakthrough?
  • How is the customer using your product today?
  • Is the team capable of building a scaled business?
  • What progress have you made till date?
  • What are the few key learnings?
  • What are your high level goals through December 2016?

    • Mail in your entries to pccecell@gmail.com

    Don't forget to include your names and contact details.
    Best of luck !
